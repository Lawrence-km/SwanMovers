Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on size, complexity, and scope. A small renovation might take 4-8 weeks, while a custom home could take 6-12 months. Commercial projects typically range from 3-18 months depending on size. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, we manage the entire permitting process as part of our comprehensive service. Our team has extensive experience navigating local building codes and regulations. We prepare and submit all necessary documentation to secure required permits and approvals, ensuring your project proceeds smoothly and complies with all legal requirements.

How do you ensure quality control during construction?

Quality control is integrated into every phase of our construction process. We employ experienced project managers who conduct regular inspections throughout the construction process. We also implement a multi-point quality checklist for each project phase, use only premium materials and trusted suppliers, and maintain open communication with clients through regular progress updates and walkthroughs.

Can you work with my existing architectural plans?

Absolutely. We regularly work with plans provided by clients or their architects. Our team will review the plans thoroughly to ensure they meet all building codes and are optimized for construction efficiency. If modifications are needed, we'll collaborate with you and your architect to implement changes while preserving the original design intent.

Do you offer warranties on your construction work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. All our projects come with a standard 1-year workmanship warranty covering any defects in construction. Many of the materials and systems we install also carry manufacturer warranties, which we pass on to you. For larger projects, we offer extended warranty options. Our commitment to quality doesn't end at project completion—we're here to ensure your satisfaction for years to come.
