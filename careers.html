Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about careers at Swan Movers Limited

What qualifications do I need to work at Swan Movers Limited?

Qualifications vary by position. For technical roles, relevant education and certifications are typically required. For skilled trades, we look for appropriate training and experience. All positions require a strong work ethic, attention to detail, and a commitment to safety and quality.

Do you offer internships or apprenticeship programs?

Yes, we offer internships for students in construction management, engineering, and design programs. We also have apprenticeship programs for various trades. These programs provide hands-on experience and mentorship from experienced professionals.

What benefits do you offer to employees?

We offer a comprehensive benefits package that includes health insurance, dental and vision coverage, retirement plans with company matching, paid time off, professional development opportunities, and performance bonuses. Specific benefits may vary based on position and employment status.

What is the work schedule like?

Work schedules vary by position and project requirements. Office staff typically work standard business hours, while field positions may have different schedules based on project timelines and weather conditions. We strive to maintain work-life balance while meeting project deadlines.

How long does the hiring process take?

The hiring process typically takes 2-4 weeks from application to offer, depending on the position and the number of candidates. We strive to keep candidates informed throughout the process and provide timely feedback after interviews.
